To format your text you can do the following. Note, quotes are auto-formatted for you.

Shortcuts:

  • Cmd+B Bold
  • Cmd+I Italic
  • Cmd+1-6 Header 1-6
  • Cmd+0 Paragraph

Text Substitutions (followed by a space):

  • 1. Ordered List
  • * Unordered List
  • - Unordered List
  • # Header 1
  • ## Header 2
  • ### Header 3, etc.
  • > Blockquote
